# Zero Reflect — Search Index

This document exists for discoverability.
It is non-normative and not part of ZR execution.

It collects phrases, questions, and concepts people search for
before they know what ZR is.

No answers are provided here.

---

## Questions People Search For

- Why does the AI refuse to answer?
- Why does the system stay silent?
- Is this a bug or a limitation?
- Why does the AI say “this is up to you”?
- Why won’t the AI decide for me?
- Why does the AI stop at this point?
- Where does automation stop?
- Why can’t responsibility be delegated further?
- Why does the AI avoid giving advice?
- Why does the response feel intentionally incomplete?
- Why does the system return nothing?
- Is silence an error or a signal?
- Why does the AI push the decision back to me?
- Why does this feel like a boundary instead of a failure?
